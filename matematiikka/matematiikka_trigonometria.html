Newtonin menetelmä

$$x_{n+1}=x_{n}-\dfrac{f\left(x_{n}\right) }{f'\left(x_{n}\right) } $$

Sekantti menetelmä

$$x_{n+1}=x_{n}-f\left( x_{n}\right) \cdot \dfrac {x_{n}-x_{n-1}}{f\left( x_{n}\right) -f\left( x_{n-1}\right) }$$

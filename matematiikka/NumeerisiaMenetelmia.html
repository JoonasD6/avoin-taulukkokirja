Numeerisia Menetelmia

Newtonin menetelmä

$$x_{n+1}=x_{n}-\dfrac{f\left(x_{n}\right) }{f'\left(x_{n}\right) } $$

Sekantti menetelmä

$$x_{n+1}=x_{n}-f\left( x_{n}\right) \cdot \dfrac {x_{n}-x_{n-1}}{f\left( x_{n}\right) -f\left(x_{n-1}\right) }$$

Taylorin polynomi

$$f\left( x\right) =\sum ^{\infty }_{n=0}=\dfrac {f^{\left( n\right) }\left( a\right) }{n!}\left( x-a\right) ^{n}$$

Puolisuunnikassääntö

$$\int ^{b}_{a}f\left( x\right) dx \approx \dfrac {h}{2}\sum ^{N}_{k=1}\left( f\left( x_{k+1}\right) +f\left( x_{k}\right) \right)$$

Kaavoja

Populaation kasvun ja kilpailun mallit

\[ \begin{array}{rl} N &=& \text{populaatiokoko} \\ K &=& \text{kantokyky} \\ r &=& \text{luontainen kasvunopeus} \\ t &=& \text{ajankohta} \\ e &=& \text{Neperin luku} \\ \theta &=& \text{kasvun kompensaatiotyyppi} \end{array} \]

Logistinen malli

\[ N_{t+1} = N_t + rN_t \Big(1-\frac{N_t}{K}\Big) \]

Rickerin malli

\[ N_{t+1} = N_te^{r\big(1-\frac{N_t}{K}\big)} \]

θ-Ricker-malli

\[ N_{t+1} = N_te^{r\big(1 - \frac{N_t}{K}\big)^\theta} \]

Hassellin malli

\[ N_{t+1} = \frac{K_1N_t}{\Big(1 + K_2N_t\Big)^\theta} \]

Beverton-Holtin malli

\[ N_{t+1} = \frac{rN_t}{1 + \frac{N_t}{K}} \]

Skellamin malli

\[ N_{t+1} = K(1 - e^{-rN_t}) \]

Maynard-Smith-Slatkinin malli

\[ N_{t+1} = \frac{K_1N_t}{1 + K_2N_t^c} \]
Brännström, Å. & Sumpter, D.J.T. 2005: The role of competition and clustering in population dynamics. — Proceedings of the Royal Society of London B: Biological Sciences 272(1576): 2065-2072.
